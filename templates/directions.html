Directions

In this website under the translate page, you have the ability to convert any text of your choosing, including any characters or numbers, to a picture of Braille, along with a 3d model of the Braille, which you will be able to 3d print. First off, you must enter text into the text box under, 'English Input'. Then, you must enter your desired dimensions for your 3d Braille model into the boxes under '3d Model Preferences'. Then, you can click the convert button. An image of your Braille will be shown below the convert button, and then you may also click the 'Download 3d Model Button' at the bottom of the page to download the 3d model. If the Braille on the 3d model extends out of the build plate, please either make the text smaller or change the parameters of the build plate length to make the Braille fit. The downloaded 3d file will be an OBJ file. This can be imported into and 3d slicer, which can then be exported to a 3d printer.
